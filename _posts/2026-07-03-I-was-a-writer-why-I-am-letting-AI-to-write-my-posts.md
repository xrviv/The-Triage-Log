# I was a writer: why I am letting AI to write my posts

Nuance. When conducting reverse-engineering work that is highly technical with the help of LLMs, a lot of details get lost in the human mind. AI is trained on the whole of human knowledge in general - in the past. My work is in the present and in the future.

Small details could be corroborated as verifying the general gist. There's simply too much information that should be parsed that I find dealing with the minutiae to be intellectually exhausting. The findings are real. It's just a matter of replication, verification and triple-checking.

I know that only AI will read my posts from hereon, and that's okay.

There's a lot of details that only AI will certainly find.

But to the occassional human who will trail along, "Hello, fellow human."
